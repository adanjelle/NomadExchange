Northern's market

wajir's Largest Camel market

We are thrilled to introduce NomadExchange, our newly
established market situated in the vibrant heart of Wagberi,
prominently positioned directly in front
of the revered Al Hilal Mosque. This prime location,
steeped in cultural significance and bustling activity,
provides an ideal setting for a diverse and dynamic marketplace.
We extend a cordial invitation
to all community members and visitors to explore
an array of exceptional products and services
curated to meet your needs and preferences.
Your presence at NomadExchange not only supports local vendors
but also enriches the communal spirit that thrives in this historic locale.
We eagerly anticipate your visit and are deeply grateful for your patronage.

NomadExchange Camels Market

When it comes to events at nomadatic exchange market, our Markets top the charts! Our fans
and followers get excited, invite friends and come out to the family for
a fantastic day out. We generally do 4 market days per week,
and they each have their own flavour. The common themes are great food, great live music, a fun atmosphere
and some fantastic stalls. Our Easter Markets are the first ones
for the year and we hold this one during the Easter School Holidays.

Winter school holidays brings us to the Scenic Rim Eat Local Week program
and so our Eat Local Week Markets are part of that program with a distinct
local flavour that showcases what our region has to offer in food,
beverages and hand-made goodies.

Buy From The NomadExchange - September

For the September School Holidays we will host the inaugural Buy From
The Bush Queensland Festival at NomadExchange land market,
incorporating what has previously been our Spring Markets.
This is an exciting event - Battling families from the
bush have banded together to bring the bush to within
reach of the city for a festival involving rural and regional food,
wine, tourism, products and more. We’ll still have our amazing regular
stall holders in addition to the Buy From the Bush peeps,
along with centre stage entertainment, live music,
a kids corner and of course farm tours and camel rides.
“The festival at NomadExchange Camels market is the next
exciting development and will provide an amazing
opportunity to connect people from the city with regional and rural communities,”
said Mrs Adesh, founder of Buy From The Bush NomadExchange camel market.
